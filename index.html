India is home to a rich variety of wildlife including Bengal tigers, Indian elephants, peacocks, and many endangered species. National parks like Jim Corbett, Kaziranga, and Ranthambore protect these majestic animals.

  • Bengal Tiger: The national animal of India.
  • Indian Elephant: Revered and often used in festivals.
  • Peacock: The national bird with beautiful plumage.
  • One-horned Rhinoceros: Found in northeastern India.
